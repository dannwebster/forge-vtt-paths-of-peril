## Soldier Moves

You get four Soldier moves. You get this one:

### ☑ Battle Scars

The war has left you with a deep physical and/or psychological wounds, (represented by your Vice) but with those wounds comes wisdom. When using your Vice to clear a Condition, gain 1 Hold that may be traded 1 for 1 to ask any Investigate! or The Third Degree! question about any situation or person encountered so far.

*Then pick three of these:*

### ☐ Crowd Control

You are an expert in fighting multiple combatants. On any Fight! move, even a failure, you always deal 1 additional Harm, but that Harm can only be applied to Mooks.

### ☐ Sniper

You are an expert at long-range target elimination. On any Shoot! move, even a failure, you always deal 1 additional Harm, but that harm can only be applied to Heavies, Bosses, and Villains.

### ☐ True Grit

You can overcome danger through sheer brawn and grit. Any time you are In Jeopardy! You can roll +Tough.

- **13+:** Unmark In Jeopardy! and clear your lowest severity Condition. If you have no Conditions, gain an Edge.
- **10-12:** Unmark In Jeopardy! and gain an Edge.
- **7-9:** Unmark In Jeopardy! but lose any Edges you have.
- **6-:** You are still In Jeopardy, but it is somehow just a little worse. The Director will tell you how.

### ☐ Tactical Genius

If given sufficient time to plan something (a Fight, a Chase, a Trap, an infiltration, etc), you may distribute an Edge to each other Hero that is involved in the planning session. That Edge is available for duration of the encounter.

### ☐ Intimidation

If are willing to rough someone up, you can roll +Tough to instead of +Charm to give someone The Third Degree! However, you never add a Contact's Rating when using Intimidation, and any result other than a 10+ you having to do them real harm to get the answer you want.

### ☐ Situational Awareness

When you Look Out!, you may roll +Nerve instead of +Sharp

### ☐ Saboteur

When you use Sneak! to infiltrate a secure area alone, roll +Nerve. 10+: gain 3 hold. 7-9: gain 1 hold. As the Director describes the infiltration and the security measures you must overcome, you may spend 1 hold to describe how you overcome the obstacle and:

- bypass a security system or guard
- leave a trap for the unwary
- disable a guard
- disable a security system you have bypassed

On a 6-, you still gain 1 Hold, but you find something unwanted along the way
