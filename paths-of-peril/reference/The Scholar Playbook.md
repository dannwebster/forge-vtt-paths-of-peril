## Shadows of the Uncanny

- ☐ **Touched by Strangeness** — When you Enlighten!, include one strange fact that is not part of real-world history. This is now true.
- ☐ **Called down Hidden Paths** — When you indulge a Virtue or Vice to clear a Condition, the Hidden World notices. You or the Director introduces a strange sign, supernatural clue, watchful interloper, etc.
- ☐ **Scarred by Trackless Wastes** — Reduce your Charm by 1 and increase your Brains by 1. Any serious conversation you have eventually bends toward the Unnatural. You may now attempt The Occult Move.
- ☐ **Bound to Forbidden Worlds** — Take the -0 Condition "Obsessed with the Uncanny" (This overrides any existing -0 Condition). This Condition cannot be cleared.
- ☐ **Lost to Darkest Legend** — Retire your character from play. Their obsession has overtaken their ordinary life, and they vanish into the Weird as a rumor, warning, relic-hunter, cultist, guardian, or legend.

## Scholar Moves

You get four Scholar moves. You get this one:

### ☑ Call Upon Your Organization

When you are in good standing with your Organization, and you have the means to contact them, once per Session, roll +Brains.

- **13+:** they provide some useful info or help in the field, and you get an Edge.
- **10-12:** they provide some useful info or help in the field.
- **7-9:** They will give you the help they ask for, but they will give you as task to accomplish as well. If you refuse or fail the task, you will be in trouble with the Organization.
- **6-:** They ask you to do something bad or give what they think you should want.

*Then pick three of these:*

### ☐ I've Read About This Sort Of Thing

Roll +Brains instead of +Nerve when you Dare!. The Director may ask you to narrate where you read about it.

### ☐ I Speak a Little of That

Chose one culture: European, Asian, African. You are fluent in all Lingua Franca of that culture. Additionally, when you encounter any language outside your fluency in any context (ancient inscription, isolated tribesman, etc), roll +Brains:

- **13+:** You speak the language like a native. You get +1 on all rolls where that language would come into play, such as Convince!, 3rd Degree!, Investigate, etc.
- **10-12:** You are fluent in the language, and get +0 on all related rolls.
- **7-9:** You have some familiarity with the language. You get -1 on all related rolls.
- **6-:** You speak a few words in the language, but will need to find an expert to do more than order coffee or find the bathroom.

### ☐ The Finer Things

You can instantly appraise almost any item's value and detect most fakes without rolling. Additionally, you can talk up a mundane item to seem rare and valuable, gaining an Edge usable on Charm rolls where a valuable item would help.

### ☐ Preparedness

When you need something unusual or rare, roll +Brains.

- **13+:** You have it, and it's perfect. Gain An Edge when using the item.
- **10+:** You have it here right now.
- **7-9:** you have it, but it is flawed or limited in some way. The Director will tell you how.
- **6-:** You know where it is, but it's somewhere real bad.

### ☐ Academic Network

Fill in one of the slots in your Contacts List with the name of your academic network, and the following values: Rating: +1, Expertise: Academia, Locale: Everywhere, Relationship: Academic Network. Whenever you need a Contact that specializes in a specific academic subject, you can declare them without rolling. For a move that allows you to upgrade the Contact, you may write that Contact's specific Name and Expertise, and set the Rating to +2.

### ☐ Expert Researcher

Any time you would Enlighten! or Investigate! using research (Director's decision), Gain 1 hold from the Investigate! Questions, even on a failure.
