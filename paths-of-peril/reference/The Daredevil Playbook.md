## Shadows of the Uncanny

- ☐ **Touched by Strangeness** — When you Enlighten!, include one strange fact that is not part of real-world history. This is now true.
- ☐ **Called down Hidden Paths** — When you indulge a Virtue or Vice to clear a Condition, the Hidden World notices. You or the Director introduces a strange sign, supernatural clue, watchful interloper, etc.
- ☐ **Scarred by Trackless Wastes** — Reduce your Charm by 1 and increase your Brains by 1. Any serious conversation you have eventually bends toward the Unnatural. You may now attempt The Occult Move.
- ☐ **Bound to Forbidden Worlds** — Take the -0 Condition "Obsessed with the Uncanny" (This overrides any existing -0 Condition). This Condition cannot be cleared.
- ☐ **Lost to Darkest Legend** — Retire your character from play. Their obsession has overtaken their ordinary life, and they vanish into the Weird as a rumor, warning, relic-hunter, cultist, guardian, or legend.

## Daredevil Moves

You get four Daredevil moves. You get this one:

### ☑ Hell on Wheels/Wings/Water

When you're vehicle or piloting your Vehicle Class in a high-pressure situation, roll +Nerve. 13+: Hold 3 and gain an Edge. 10+: Hold 3. 7-9: Hold 1. 6-: Hold 1, but you put some strain on your vehicle. The Director will tell you how.

You may spend 1 hold to do one of the following:
- Add or subtract 1 Segment from a Chase
- Unmark In Jeopardy! for anyone in the vehicle
- Avoid one external danger (a burst of gunfire, a collision, etc)
- Escape one pursuing vehicle
- Maintain control of the vehicle
- Impress, dismay or frighten someone

*Then pick three of these:*

### ☐ Ace of Engines

Take a second Vehicle Class Specialization. You also have a second Starting Vehicle.

### ☐ No Guts, No Glory

When you dive straight into danger without hedging your bets, you get an Edge. If you take a durable Condition, mark experience.

### ☐ Vehicular Homicide

Any time you want to cause Harm with a vehicle, including using vehicle-mounted weapons, you deal +1 Harm, even on a failure.

### ☐ Gearhead

You are an expert in all things mechanical. When trying to fix or sabotage a mechanical device, roll +Nerve. 13+: Hold 3 and gain an Edge. 10+: Hold 3 7-9: Hold 1. On a 6-: Hold 1 but the Director will tell you how something goes unexpectedly wrong.

You may spend 1 Hold one for one to one of the following:
- You make something broken work unreliably
- You make something unreliable work perfectly
- You make something in perfect working order work better than ever
- You learn something unexpected. Ask an Investigate! Question
- You make your changes undetectable or undetectably
- You make it malfunction or self-destruct unreliably at some specified time in the future

### ☐ Cool as Ice

In any situation where your dash and flair might impress someone (Director's call), you can roll +Nerve instead of +Charm to Convince!

### ☐ Intuitive Navigator

You are never lost. You always know where you are and you know how to get where you want to go. You get an Edge in any situation (such as a Chase) where orientation or knowing your way could help.
